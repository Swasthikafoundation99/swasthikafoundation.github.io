About Us

The Swasthika Sevayan Foundation is dedicated to promoting public health, wellness, tribal health initiatives, women's empowerment, and education across rural and under-served areas of India. We believe in inclusive growth and sustainable development through dedicated services.

  • Community awarness, Heatlh education & empowerment
